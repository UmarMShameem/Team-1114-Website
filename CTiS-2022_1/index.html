Social Media Algorithms Website

This website is intended to explore social media algorithms in detail, as well as its impact on its users.

Areas covered include the definition of the topic, the opportunities of algorithms, the risks they pose, what choices there are to avoid harmful consequences, as well as ethical reflections made by each individual group member.
